# Enterprise Advisory Layers

ProjectRAG includes early stubs for future enterprise advisory roles:

- Artificial CTO
- Artificial Architect
- Artificial COO
- Artificial CEO

These layers are **recommendation-only**. They do not approve, execute, or enforce changes.

## Purpose

The enterprise layer is intended to translate GraphRAG, digital twin, prediction, security, and operational context into human-readable recommendations for different decision levels.

## Future Use

### Artificial CTO

Technical strategy recommendations:

- platform direction
- reliability investment
- model/runtime strategy
- engineering risk prioritization

### Artificial Architect

Architecture recommendations:

- service boundaries
- dependency simplification
- topology improvements
- migration planning

### Artificial COO

Operational recommendations:

- incident response process
- runbook quality
- backup and recovery posture
- operational readiness

### Artificial CEO

Executive recommendations:

- business impact summaries
- strategic tradeoffs
- risk posture
- investment priorities

## Safety Boundary

All enterprise modules must remain advisory unless a future explicit governance model is designed.

Current mode:

```text
recommendation_only
execution_disabled
```

No autonomous decisions are allowed.
